About Me

I'm Tatiparthi Sunny, a passionate UI/UX Designer and Full Stack Developer with 4+ years of experience creating digital products that users love. My journey in tech began with a fascination for how design and code intersect to create meaningful experiences.

What sets me apart is my dual expertise in both design and development. I don't just create beautiful interfaces - I understand how to implement them efficiently. This unique combination allows me to bridge the gap between design vision and technical implementation, ensuring pixel-perfect results that are both visually stunning and technically sound.

My approach combines user-centered design principles with cutting-edge development practices. Whether working on a sleek mobile app or a complex web platform, I bring the same attention to detail and commitment to excellence.
